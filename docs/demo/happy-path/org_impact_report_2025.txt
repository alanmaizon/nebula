Harbor Community Network
Program Impact Report - 2025

Organization profile:
- 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving East and North Portland neighborhoods.
- Primary focus: housing stability, food access, and case navigation for low-income families.

Need and population served:
- In 2025, 62% of enrolled households reported being at risk of eviction within 90 days.
- 71% of participating households had at least one child under age 18.
- 68% of client households identified transportation barriers as a reason for missed appointments.

Service delivery outcomes:
- 412 households enrolled in case management across 2025.
- 289 households remained stably housed for at least six months after intake.
- 198 households increased monthly income through benefits enrollment or employment placement.
- 146 youth received after-school tutoring tied to attendance recovery plans.

Evaluation approach:
- Monthly case file audits conducted by the program manager.
- Participant outcome survey administered at intake, 90 days, and 180 days.
- Dashboard tracks housing stability, attendance, and benefit enrollment milestones.

Community partnerships:
- Three neighborhood schools provide referral pathways.
- Two food distribution partners provide weekly coordinated support.
