Attachment B: Program Timeline
Project period: July 2026 - June 2027

Phase 1: Launch and enrollment (July - September 2026)
- Finalize referral protocol with school and clinic partners.
- Enroll 120 households into coordinated case management.
- Complete baseline assessments for housing risk and service barriers.

Phase 2: Service delivery and stabilization (October 2026 - March 2027)
- Deliver weekly case navigation sessions.
- Issue transportation and emergency support vouchers.
- Monitor participant progress in monthly interdisciplinary case review meetings.

Phase 3: Outcomes measurement and closeout (April - June 2027)
- Conduct 90-day and 180-day participant outcome follow-up.
- Analyze program outcomes and prepare final report.
- Submit grant closeout narrative and budget summary.
