Funding Opportunity: City of Portland Community Resilience Microgrant 2026
Funder: City of Portland Office of Community Partnerships
Deadline: March 15, 2026 at 5:00 PM Pacific

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be nonprofit organizations with active 501(c)(3) status.
- Applicants must provide services within Portland city limits.
- Applicants must have at least one completed program cycle in the last 24 months.

Questions:
1. Need Statement (350 words max): Describe the specific community need your program addresses and who is most affected.
2. Program Design (400 words max): Explain your activities, implementation timeline, and how participants are served.
3. Outcomes and Evaluation (300 words max): Identify measurable outcomes and how progress will be tracked.

Required Attachments:
- Attachment A: Project Budget
- Attachment B: Program Timeline
- Include two letters of community support

Rubric and Scoring Criteria:
- Need alignment and urgency (30 points)
- Feasibility and readiness (30 points)
- Outcomes and evidence quality (25 points)
- Budget reasonableness (15 points)

Disallowed costs:
- Purchase of real estate
- Political campaign activity
- Expenses unrelated to direct program delivery
