Community Support Letters - Excerpts

Letter 1: Alder Street Elementary Family Services Team
"Eastside Family Bridge supported 96 families referred by our school in the last year.
Their case managers provide consistent bilingual communication and practical housing navigation."

Letter 2: Sunrise Community Health Clinic
"The program's transportation vouchers reduced missed behavioral health appointments for shared clients.
We observed faster follow-through on care plans when families were co-enrolled in housing stabilization."

Letter 3: East County Food Access Coalition
"Our pantry network coordinates weekly referrals with Eastside Family Bridge.
Families receiving coordinated food and housing support had fewer repeat emergency requests over six months."
