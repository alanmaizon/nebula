Attachment B: Program Timeline
Project period: July 2027 - June 2028

Phase 1: Launch and enrollment (July - September 2027)
- Hire and onboard two case coordinators by July 15.
- Finalize referral workflows with three schools, two clinics, and one housing court navigator.
- Enroll first cohort of 110 households and complete baseline assessments.

Phase 2: Intensive service delivery (October 2027 - March 2028)
- Provide weekly case management sessions for active participants.
- Operate monthly legal aid clinics focused on eviction prevention.
- Issue transportation vouchers within 48 hours for approved appointments.
- Run monthly interdisciplinary case reviews with partner agencies.

Phase 3: Outcome tracking and closeout (April - June 2028)
- Conduct 90-day and 180-day follow-up checks for all active households.
- Reconcile performance dashboard metrics against case records.
- Submit final narrative, outcomes table, and fiscal closeout package.
