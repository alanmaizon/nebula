Funding Opportunity: Multnomah County Family Stability Grant 2027
Funder: Multnomah County Department of Human Services
Deadline: April 30, 2027 at 4:00 PM Pacific

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be nonprofit organizations with active 501(c)(3) status.
- Applicants must provide direct services within Multnomah County, Oregon.
- Applicants must have completed at least one full 12-month program cycle in the last 24 months.

Questions:
1. Need Statement (350 words max): Describe the specific community need your program addresses, who is most affected, and why this need is urgent in your service area.
2. Program Design (400 words max): Explain core activities, staffing model, implementation timeline, and how participants are served.
3. Outcomes and Evaluation (300 words max): Identify measurable outcomes, targets, data collection methods, and how progress is tracked and reported.

Required Attachments:
- Attachment A: Project Budget
- Attachment B: Program Timeline
- Include two letters of community support

Rubric and Scoring Criteria:
- Need alignment and urgency (30 points)
- Feasibility and readiness (30 points)
- Outcomes and evidence quality (25 points)
- Budget reasonableness (15 points)

Disallowed costs:
- Purchase of real estate
- Political campaign activity
- Expenses unrelated to direct program delivery
