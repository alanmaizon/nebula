Evaluation Framework Appendix C

Primary outcomes and targets for the 2027-2028 grant period:
- Housing Stability Rate: target 75% of enrolled households remain stably housed at 180 days.
- Income or Benefits Gain: target 52% of enrolled households increase monthly income or complete a new benefits enrollment.
- Appointment Adherence: target 72% of participants attend at least 80% of scheduled appointments.

Data sources:
- Case management system records (housing status, service contacts, referrals).
- Benefits verification logs and employment follow-up forms.
- Appointment attendance logs from partner clinics and schools.

Measurement cadence:
- Baseline at intake.
- Follow-up at day 90 and day 180.
- Monthly dashboard updates for internal quality checks.
- Quarterly summary reports to county grant staff.

Quality controls:
- Supervisors audit 10% of case records each month.
- Discrepancies are corrected within five business days.
- Indicator definitions are version-controlled to prevent metric drift across reporting periods.
