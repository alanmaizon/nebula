Organization: BrightSteps Community Network

2024 Impact Snapshot
- 1,482 youth served across three districts.
- 68% completed job-readiness training.
- 54% placed into paid internships within 90 days.
- 72% of placed participants retained employment for at least 6 months.

Identified Barriers
- Limited transportation access for evening shifts.
- Low digital literacy among first-time job seekers.
- High dropout risk during first 45 days of placement.

Existing Program Assets
- 24 employer partners in retail, logistics, and healthcare support.
- Mobile coaching team with bilingual staff.
- Case management system with monthly progress tracking.

Planned 2026 Targets
- Serve 1,700 youth.
- Reach 60% paid internship placement.
- Improve 6-month retention to 75%.
