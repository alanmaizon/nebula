Funding Opportunity: City of Dublin Youth Workforce Innovation Grant 2026

Program Overview
The City invites nonprofit proposals to expand paid career pathways for youth ages 16-24 in underserved neighborhoods.
Total fund available: EUR 1,200,000. Maximum request per applicant: EUR 180,000.
Project period: July 1, 2026 to June 30, 2027.

Required Narrative Questions
1) Need Statement (max 300 words)
Describe the local employment barriers for target youth and provide evidence from 2024-2025 program data.

2) Program Design (max 500 words)
Explain participant recruitment, employer partnerships, and monthly service model.

3) Outcomes and Evaluation (max 400 words)
Define at least three measurable outcomes, collection methods, and reporting cadence.

4) Sustainability (max 250 words)
Describe how services continue after grant period.

Submission Requirements
- Attachment A: Line-item budget with justification
- Attachment B: Organization board list
- Attachment C: Two employer support letters
- Include implementation timeline by quarter

Scoring Rubric
- Community Need and Alignment: 30 points
- Feasibility and Operations: 30 points
- Outcomes and Measurement: 25 points
- Budget Reasonableness: 15 points

Restrictions
- No alcohol, entertainment, or lobbying expenses.
- Equipment purchases above EUR 10,000 require prior approval.
- Indirect costs capped at 12%.
